About

Exploring the intersection of human expertise and AI assistance in software development. This portfolio showcases projects built using hybrid human-LLM development methodology, with transparent contribution tracking.

Every commit in my projects is labeled to indicate the development approach used, from traditional hand-coded implementations to AI-assisted pair programming and LLM-architected solutions. This transparency provides insight into the evolving landscape of software development.

The goal is not to replace human expertise with AI, but to explore how human judgment and AI capabilities can complement each other to build better software more efficiently.
